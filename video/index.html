The problem
AI bidding agents leak their valuations.
On a public mempool, every signed transaction carries its bid amount in cleartext. An autonomous agent broadcasts its max valuation to anyone scraping the chain.
The cost is structural — anyone can outbid by a single dollar and capture the entire surplus.

The solution
Sealed bids, attested by hardware.
Sealdex moves the bidding window into MagicBlock's Private Ephemeral Rollup. Bid amounts are encrypted inside Intel TDX hardware until the seller calls reveal — and only the winner is committed back to base Solana. Losing bids are discarded without disclosure.

Architecture
Four instructions. One enclave.
Anchor program on Solana base layer. Auction and Bid accounts are immediately delegated to a TEE validator and stay sealed until settlement.
01
create_auction
Base layer
Seller posts the lot, init Auction PDA + permission, delegate to TEE validator.
02
place_bid
Base → TEE
Bid PDA created with sealed amount, delegated to TEE in the same transaction.
03
settle_auction
In-TEE
Iterate bids inside the enclave, find max, commit winner back to base Solana.
04
claim_lot
Base layer
Winner claims, escrow agent triggers payment via Private Payments API.
